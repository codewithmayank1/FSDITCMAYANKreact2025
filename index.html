Doctor Strange

Doctor Strange ka asli naam Stephen Strange hai. Pehle wo ek mashhoor neurosurgeon tha, lekin accident ke baad uske haath kharab ho gaye. Ilaaj ki talash me usne Ancient One se magic seekha aur ban gaya Sorcerer Supreme, jo dharti ko mystic dangers se bachata hai.

Powers

  • Mystic Arts
  • Time Manipulation (Eye of Agamotto)
  • Teleportation & Portals
  • Astral Projection

  2. Avengers: Infinity War (2018)
  3. Avengers: Endgame (2019)
  4. Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness (2022)
